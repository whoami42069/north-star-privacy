Delete Your Account

Last updated: March 1, 2026

If you would like to delete your North Star account and all associated data, you can do so by following the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to support@northstar-alerts.com from the email address associated with your North Star account.
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. We will verify your identity and process the deletion within 30 days.

What Data Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove the following data:

  • Account profile — email, display name, and authentication credentials
  • Price alerts — all alarms and alert configurations you created
  • Watchlist — all saved symbols and watchlist entries
  • Push notification tokens — FCM device tokens stored for delivering alerts
  • Subscription data — premium subscription status and payment references
Please note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will lose access to all your saved alerts and watchlist items.

Data Retention After Deletion

After processing your deletion request:

  • All personal data listed above is permanently deleted from our database within 30 days.
  • Anonymized crash reports collected by Firebase Crashlytics may be retained for up to 90 days for diagnostic purposes. These cannot be linked back to your account.
  • If you have an active Google Play subscription, you must cancel it separately through the Google Play Store. We cannot cancel subscriptions on your behalf.
